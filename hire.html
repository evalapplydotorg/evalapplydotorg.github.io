Build You A B2B "Micro SaaS"

Do you own a B2B business and want to build B2B SaaS product?

  • Help you discover? The "microsaasidea.com" newsletter looks pretty cool (it's a paid service and I have no affiliation). Options like that abound on the glorious world wide web! But it's easy to go around in circles of analysis paralysis. Sometimes one just needs to think out loud to another human. Somebody who is unfettered by one's own priors and biases. Who will listen without prejudice. Who is candid. I'm happy to be that body on a short call, as rubber duck as well as Socratic Method-ist, to help you rummage through your brain.

  • Help you decide? Upon gaining clarity on exactly the SaaS business to create, doing some good ol' Build versus Buy trade-off is essential. Perhaps a from-scratch application is the right answer. Perhaps it's better to white-label an existing product. Perhaps it is better to productize some other aspect of your business without building custom software.

  • Build your MVP? If you've decided to build, I can rapid-prototype a minimum viable product that you can put in front of prospects and validate your idea. I do full-systems work, using a curated stack of open-source technologies I know and trust.

  • Email me, whatever you choose.
