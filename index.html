I build B2B SaaS 3 products and engineering systems. I strive to build better without burning out. To that end, I hold these things dear.

  • Culture. Foster a generative culture that yields elite outcomes.
  • Systems. Build leveraged systems that help do more with less.

  • Mind. Keep my sanity through inevitable dark tea times of the soul.
  • Craft. Software architecture, mental models, developer experience.
  • Making! Serious things. Silly things. Fun things. Nerdy things.

If challenges around these rob you of opportunity, progress, and sleep, I am available to help, through one of these initiatives.

  • Deploy writing strategically. Good writing habits transform 1x product engineering teams into the proverbial 10x teams 4. Yet few teams choose to build a solid culture of writing and reading. Their people toil on hamster wheels of endless forgetting and rework. Sad. We can do better.
  • Advance product engineering maturity. Hiring more people makes late projects later. Death marches burn away minds and bodies. Mass layoffs destroy capital. Intelligent professional coaching, teaching, and on-boarding is how teams get very good, stay lean, attract and retain the right kind of people, and make big things happen. Let's bet on our people.
